In the next chapters, you'll learn how to power up your ecommerce development and store with AI assistants and Large Language Models (LLMs). These tools can help you build your store faster, get assistance in your development, and even learn Medusa interactively.

## What Makes Medusa AI-Friendly?

Medusa's architecture and customization capabilities make it an ideal platform for leveraging AI assistants and LLMs in your commerce development workflow.

A common issue with traditional commerce platforms is that they aren't built to support AI assistants and LLMs, leading to unreliable implementations, security risks, and AI not understanding the platform's architecture. This is often due to hacky workarounds, rigid architecture, or lack of protected yet flexible customization capabilities.

On the other hand, Medusa's architecture, Framework, and AI tools support developers and non-technical store owners in using AI assistants to build stores efficiently and securely, and go live faster. With Medusa, you can leverage AI assistants and LLMs to build your store, get assistance in your development, and even learn Medusa interactively.

---

## What Can You Build with Medusa and AI Assistants?

By levaging AI assistants in your Medusa development workflow, you can:

1. Build unique features in your store that are tailored to your business needs efficiently, without worrying about the complexities of the platform's architecture.
2. Get assistance in your development, such as debugging issues, optimizing performance, deployment, and more.
3. Build storefronts that follow best practices for commerce and provide a great user experience.
